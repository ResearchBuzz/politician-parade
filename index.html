Find Congressional Representatives in News and Web Results: Politician Parade

Politician Parade adds a state's Congresspeople to your news or Web search. Depending on how many reps your state has, this tool will generate one or more URLs with your query AND your Congressional representatives added as an "or" array.







You'll need an API Key to use this tool. You can get them free from https://www.propublica.org/datastore/api/propublica-congress-api .
